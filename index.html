Odessey and Oracle

The Zombies

April 19, 1968

Odessey and Oracle was released in 1968 by English band The Zombies. Recordings took place in London at Abbey Road and Olympic Studios over the period of three months. This is the band's second studio album and has been ranked number 100 on Rolling Stone magazine's list of the 500 Greatest Albums of All Time.

Band Members and Producers

  • Colin Blunstone - Lead Vocals
  • Rod Argent - Organ; Piano; Harpsichord; Mellotron
  • Pat Atkinson - Guitar
  • Chris White - Bass Guitar
  • Hugh Grundy - Drums
